Annual Project: Support High School Students in DR Congo

Each year, Africa Virtual Education (AVE) launches an Annual Project focused on a specific educational need. This year, we are supporting high school students in the Democratic Republic of Congo who face severe financial difficulties that threaten their ability to continue their studies.

Join us by donating, partnering, or volunteering. Together, we can bring hope and education to the next generation of African leaders.

Quick Project Snapshot

  • Focus: High school students in DR Congo
  • Needs: Tuition, laptops, study materials
  • Approach: Mentorship + financial support

Our Mission

At Africa Virtual Education, our mission is to empower students across Africa by providing access to mentorship, education support, and learning opportunities. We believe that everyone deserves a fair chance to build their future through education, and we work hand in hand with members, partners, and donors to make that possible.

“Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world.” — Nelson Mandela (South Africa)

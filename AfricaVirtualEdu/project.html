Annual Project

Each year, Africa Virtual Education (AVE) launches a new educational initiative focused on supporting learners in need. Our current project supports high school students in DR Congo facing financial hardship.

Project Objective

Provide tuition, laptops, and mentorship support to more than 100 students.

How It Works

We identify students in need through local partners and schools, then provide a combination of financial aid, equipment, and mentorship.
